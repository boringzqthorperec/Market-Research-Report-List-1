<p><p>The Automotive Real-time Clocks (RTCs) market is segmented into several types based on communication protocols and power efficiency. I2C-bus RTCs utilize the Inter-Integrated Circuit bus for communication, ideal for space-constrained applications. SPI RTCs employ the Serial Peripheral Interface for faster data transfer. Integrated Quartz Crystal RTCs leverage built-in quartz components for precise timekeeping. Ultra-Low-Power RTCs are designed for energy efficiency, making them suitable for applications requiring minimal power consumption, such as in electric vehicles and advanced driver assistance systems.</p></p>
