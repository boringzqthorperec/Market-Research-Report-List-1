<p><ul><li>Self-supporting Technology</li><li>Self-sealing Technology</li><li>Auxiliary-supported Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive run-flat tires market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Self-supporting Technology**: These tires have reinforced sidewalls that maintain shape and support the vehicle even when deflated.</p><p>2. **Self-sealing Technology**: This type features a sealant layer inside that automatically seals punctures, allowing the tire to remain inflated temporarily.</p><p>3. **Auxiliary-supported Technology**: These tires include an additional support ring, which acts as a backup to carry the vehicle when the tire is deflated. </p><p>Together, these technologies enhance safety and convenience for drivers.</p></p>
