<p><p>Cloud infrastructure services market encompasses three primary types: Public, Private, and Hybrid Clouds. Public Cloud services are offered over the internet by third-party providers, accessible to multiple users and typically cost-effective. Private Clouds are dedicated to a single organization, providing greater control and security for sensitive data. Hybrid Clouds blend both models, allowing businesses to leverage the benefits of public and private infrastructures, facilitating flexibility while maintaining control over critical applications and data management.</p></p>
