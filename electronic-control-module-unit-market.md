<p><p>The Electronic Control Module Unit market comprises various types designed for specific vehicle functions. The Central Control Module (CCM) integrates multiple vehicle systems, while the Central Timing Module (CTM) manages lighting and alarms. The General Electronic Module (GEM) oversees various electronic functions, and the Body Control Module (BCM) manages body-related features. The Suspension Control Module (SCM) optimizes ride quality, and others encompass additional specialized control units.</p></p>
