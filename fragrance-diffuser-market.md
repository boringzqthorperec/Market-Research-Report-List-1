<p><p>The fragrance diffuser market includes several types, each utilizing different methods to disperse essential oils. Ultrasonic diffusers use sound waves to create a fine mist, blending water and oil for a longer-lasting aroma. Evaporative diffusers employ a fan to circulate air through a pad or wick, rapidly evaporating the oils. Electric heat diffusers warm the oils to release their scent, while other categories may include candle-based or reed diffusers, offering a variety of ways to enhance indoor environments with fragrance.</p></p>
