<p><ul><li>Spray Drying</li><li>Freeze Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Maqui berry powder market is primarily categorized into two drying methods: spray drying and freeze drying. Spray drying involves rapidly dehydrating the berry puree using hot air, which preserves color and flavor while producing a fine powder, making it cost-effective for large-scale production. Conversely, freeze drying maintains more of the berry's nutrients and freshness by freezing it and then removing moisture under vacuum conditions. Both methods cater to different consumer preferences for quality and pricing in the health food sector.</p></p>
