<p><ul><li>Low Speed System Oil</li><li>Middle Speed System Oil</li><li>High Speed System Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine system oil market is categorized into three types based on engine speeds: low speed, middle speed, and high speed. Low speed system oils are designed for large, slow-turning engines, typically found in bulk carriers and tankers, offering superior lubrication and efficiency. Middle speed oils cater to medium-speed engines, balancing performance and fuel economy, commonly used in ferries and cargo vessels. High speed system oils are formulated for fast-running engines, such as those in smaller vessels, emphasizing rapid response and cleanliness.</p></p>
