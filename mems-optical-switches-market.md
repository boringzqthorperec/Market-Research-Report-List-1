<p><p>The MEMS optical switches market is segmented into single-mode and multimode optical switches. MEMS single-mode optical switches are designed to transmit signals efficiently over long distances with minimal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and data centers. In contrast, MEMS multimode optical switches support multiple light paths, offering higher data rates over shorter distances, suitable for local area networks and short-range applications. Both types leverage micro-electromechanical systems technology for compact, reliable, and fast signal routing in optical networks.</p></p>
