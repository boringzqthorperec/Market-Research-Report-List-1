<p><strong>The Oil & Gas Biocides Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxidizing Fungicide</li><li>Non-Oxidizing Fungicide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Oil & Gas Biocides Market includes two main types: oxidizing fungicides and non-oxidizing fungicides. Oxidizing fungicides, such as chlorine and hydrogen peroxide, work by releasing oxygen to destroy microbial cells, making them effective against a wide range of pathogens. Non-oxidizing fungicides, like quaternary ammonium compounds and isothiazolinones, inhibit microbial growth through different mechanisms, often providing residual protection. Both types are crucial for maintaining operational integrity and preventing biofouling in oil and gas production and transportation systems.</p></p>
