<p><p>The UHD surgical monitors market includes two primary types: LED UHD displays and LCD UHD displays. LED UHD displays utilize light-emitting diodes, offering superior brightness, contrast, and color accuracy, enhancing visibility during surgeries. In contrast, LCD UHD displays rely on liquid crystal technology, providing reliable performance and good image quality, often at a lower cost. Both types are integral for delivering high-resolution images crucial for surgical procedures, enabling better visualization and precision for surgeons and medical teams.</p></p>
